Welcome

Mebrahtu Fisseha Weldeghebriel

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Geosciences at Princeton University. My research focuses on reconstructing the long-term evolution of Earth’s ocean chemistry and climate using lithium isotopes, fluid inclusions in marine halite and carbonates, and numerical mass-balance modeling.
