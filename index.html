Mebrahtu Fisseha Weldeghebriel

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Geosciences at Princeton University. I study how Earth’s ocean chemistry and climate have changed over millions of years by combining paleocean chemistry, paleoclimatology, and the geochemistry of chemical sediments with numerical modeling to reconstruct ancient environmental conditions.
